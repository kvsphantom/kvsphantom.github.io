This experiment aims at studying the effect of loudness on the minimum perceivable difference in frequencies of two tones played one after another.

Approximate Time Required : < 10 minutes

PAGE 1 : Follow the instructions below for calibrating loudness. Headphones are strongly recommended!

PAGE 2: In next page, buttons in Fixed Frequencies column play the same tone whenever the button is clicked. The sliders control and play the second tone for comparison. More the slider level, more the frequency of the tone being played. Reduce the slider till you can barely hear the difference in frequency between the fixed frequency tone and the corresponding slider.



                   

CALIBRATE THE LOUDNESS

Calibrate the loudness level. Reduce the volume using the slider till you can barely hear the tone. NOTE: Please do not change the system volume at a later stage. Required for truthful results!

PLEASE USE HEADPHONES!
